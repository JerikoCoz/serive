Market Place

Realtor

A popular real estate website that offers listings for homes to buy, sell, or rent across the U.S. It provides detailed property information, neighborhood insights, market trends, and mortgage calculators.

trulia

Is a real estate website that helps users explore homes for sale, rent, or purchase in the U.S. It offers property listings along with neighborhood information, crime maps, school ratings, and local amenities, providing a comprehensive view of each area.

Zillow

A leading real estate platform in the U.S. that offers listings for buying, selling, and renting homes. It provides property details, estimated home values (Zestimates), and tools like mortgage calculators and affordability assessments.
